Frequently asked questions

What is Remote all about?

Remote is a workspace platform connecting Advertiser and Earner. Advertiser are does that want to grow their social media presence by posing tasks for earner to perform eg. following of accounts,liking of their posts etc. and the Earner are does that we paid by doing simple tasks on social media eg. following accounts,liking of posts etc..

How much can i make on remote?

There is no limit on the amount you can earn on SocialEarning. You can work and earn as much as you want, many freelancers come to SocialEarning to earn full time while others earn part-time utilizing their free time. All you have to do is ensure you add all your social media accounts and they are verified. You also earn more money when you engage in referrals which gives you 300 naira on each user you refer and they created account as earner and 0.5% on first deposit of each user you refer and created account as poster. And also stay active on the site to perform more tasks.

When can i withdraw my money?

You eligible to withdraw your money anytime you reach the minimum withdraw amount which is 1000 naira (NO REFERRAL NEEDED). And your withdraw will be send to your selected added bank accounts immediately. if you can't receive your money after 24hrs kindly contact the admin.
